Hi! I'm **Luis** (in the tech world I go by LuisPa). I describe myself as an aspiring polymath, father, technologist, software developer, and artist. I spent years working at tech corporations and am now focused on a couple of startups and consulting on projects.

I've worked with many technologies, and lately I've been learning and having fun diving deep into **cybersecurity** and **artificial intelligence** — fascinating and intriguing worlds.

As a child I dreamed of studying mathematics or fine arts, and after many years I ended up merging them both. I enjoy combining engineering, art, and strategy to create useful systems. As a constant learner, on this blog you'll find a mix of rigor and creativity. I hope you enjoy these **technical notes** on operating systems, home automation, networking, software development, and artificial intelligence.

About other facets and passions: [software development](https://github.com/LuisPalacios), my [drawings](https://www.instagram.com/luis.palacios.derqui), and my [professional background](https://www.linkedin.com/in/luis-palacios-derqui/).
